Our Campaigns

Read about our campaigns for guaranteed healthcare and affordable housing in our region.

Guaranteed Healthcare

A tight clique of private health insurers and drug manufacturers are conspiring to extract the greatest profit margins from the ill, while offering the least amount of care possible. This system, which treats patients as customers and their life-saving treatments as mere commodities, leaves America's health care system the most expensive on the planet, with less coverage and more medical bankruptcies than any other country in the developed world. While action to abolish this inhumane system on the national level stalls, Hudson Valley Democratic Socialists supports the New York Health Act, a bill currently in the State Senate that would establish guaranteed healthcare coverage in New York State.

Affordable Housing

While the lion's share of outreach and activism from Hudson Valley Democratic Socialists concerns the campaign for guaranteed healthcare in New York, we also work actively for fair, accessible and affordable housing in the region. We're proud of our work to protect tenants' rights and promote rent stabilization programs in the Hudson Valley, and we'll continue to push for a more liveable community.
